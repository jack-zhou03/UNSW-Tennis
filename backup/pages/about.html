About Us


The UNSW Tennis Club always has a strong presence on campus right from when the Sports Association was first formed back in 1953, four years after the University of New South Wales was established. As one of the seven clubs named as its foundation members, the club has maintained its stature among the now 40-strong clubs run under the Sports Association. In fact, some members have been regularly participating in club activities for over 15 years now!

Social tennis has remained as popular as ever as it caters to all levels of players from absolute first-timers to long-time "pros". We also have an internal yearly club championship as well as fielding teams for external competitions all year long for the more competitive players, catering to a range of skill levels. The external competitions includes Eastern Suburbs Tennis Competition and intervarsity competitions such as the Eastern Conference Games and the Australian University Games where we have had great success in the past.

Whether you are thinking of picking up your racquet for the first time or have been playing tennis since you started walking, you will always find an activity suitable for your standards here in the Tennis Club!

So please keep checking back for the latest news on events, facilities and services!
