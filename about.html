About Us


The Tennis Club is one of the founding members of the Sports Association of UNSW and has been successfully bringing tennis to both students and non-students at the university for a long time.

Social tennis has proved by far the most popular event we organise, and is an excellent opportunity for members to meet other members !!! Just ask the many long-time members who have formed lasting friendships and still frequent the tennis courts on Sunday despite having graduated years ago!

Apart from social tennis, we offer more competitive opportunities, ranging from social competitions with the Eastern Suburbs Tennis Competition, to national-level sports such as the Intervarsity Games.

For a more detailed look at our activities, please refer to the latest newsletters.
