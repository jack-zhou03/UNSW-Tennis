Racquet and balls will be provided, but you can bring your own if preferred.

Cardio Tennis


Cardio Tennis is a fun, social, group tennis-fitness program for people of all fitness levels and tennis abilities. It's more about getting a great, high-energy workout than pure tennis technique. Constant movement is the focus as participants are guided through a series of fun and heart-pumping drills. Participants wear heart rate monitors, exercise to music, use low-compression balls and a variety of equipment, including agility ladders. All equipment provided except Heart Rate Monitors. It is recommended you bring your own HR Monitor. HR Monitor can be purchased at a discounted rate through the instructor.

All instructors are Tennis Australia Qualified Coaches as well as Registered Cardio Tennis Coaches.

Note: Cardio Tennis is a fitness class focusing on high-energy workout instead of technical fundamentals. Participants looking for improving their tennis skills should enrol in the Beginner's Class.

To find out more about Cardio Tennis visit http://cardiotennis.com.au/why-cardio/

What to bring/wear: Bring water and towel, and wear comfortable clothes and sports shoes. No tennis racquet needed.

Important Course Information:

  1. Wear comfortable clothing, sport shoes and bring water
  2. Apply sunscreen 30 minutes before class (for day classes)
  3. Please aim to be early so that the class can start on time Medical issues which may interfere with class participation should be discussed with instructor prior to first lesson
  4. You will be informed of any updates or wet weather class cancellation by email or SMS
  5. Maximum of 2 weeks make up classes will be provided at the end of the course in the event of classes being cancelled due to wet weather.
  6. No classes will be held on public holidays or semester break.

Refund and Transfer Policy

Please choose carefully as we do not refund unless the course is cancelled or unless there is proof of extenuating circumstances where it is not possible for student to participate in course. A transfer to a different class may be granted prior to course commencement date. No transfer is allowable between classes once the course has commenced. No make up class will be provided to individuals for any class missed unless the class is cancelled due to wet weather.
